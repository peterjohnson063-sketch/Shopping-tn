About Shopping.TN

Born in the heart of Tunisia's Sahel region, Shopping.TN was created to connect the world with the extraordinary craftsmanship of Monastir, Ksar Hellal, and beyond.

🇹🇳
Our Mission

Sahel to the World

Tunisia's Sahel region has been producing world-class furniture and crafts for centuries. Shopping.TN is building the digital bridge that connects these master artisans with customers across 50+ countries.

Every purchase directly supports a Tunisian family, preserves a traditional craft, and keeps alive a 500-year-old heritage.
